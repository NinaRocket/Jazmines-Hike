Jazmine's Journey

About Jazmine's Journey

We are a group of volunteers with a passion for improving the lives of families impacted by childhood cancer and raising funds for pediatric cancer research. Our dream is to find a cure for childhood cancer and better treatments until that dream is realized. Amber and Michael know first hand the struggles that families are faced with during treatments and want to see other families going through this have all the support and resources they need. We also want to see children going through treatments have a bit of joy along the way! That is what we are here for and that is how your support makes it a reality.


Board Members

Amber Reiter

President

Jazmine’s Mom, the President of Jazmine’s Journey. She is wife and a mother to seven children who enjoys hiking, crafting, photography, and the outdoors. My mission is to support families facing similar battles, raise awareness about pediatric cancer, and work towards a future where no family has to endure the heartbreak of losing a child to this disease.With a focus on providing emotional, financial, and practical support to families, I lead our dedicated team in striving to make a tangible difference. Together, we aim to bring hope, resources, and comfort to those who need it most, while also advocating for a cure.
