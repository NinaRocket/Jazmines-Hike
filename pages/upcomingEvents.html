October 25, 2025 7am to 10am

Jazmine’s Hike

Location: North Mountain Visitor Center 12950 N 7th St, Phoenix, AZ 85022

Join us for Jazmine’s Hike: Her Unseen Journey to Cure Pediatric Cancer on Saturday, October 25th, from 7 AM to 10 AM at 12950 N 7th St, Phoenix, AZ 85022. We are taking the steps that Jazmine couldn’t, raising funds for Steele Children’s Cancer Research, with 100% of proceeds donated to help advance pediatric cancer research.
⛰️ Registration: $20 per participant

☕ Enjoy complimentary coffee! Thanks to our generous sponsor, Serafina Coffee Roasters, all attendees can start their morning with a fresh cup of coffee. Every step we take is a step toward hope. Join us as we honor Jazmine’s memory, support children battling cancer, and come together as a community for a great cause.

🚨 Disclaimer: Participants acknowledge that they are voluntarily taking part in this event and assume all risks associated with hiking, including but not limited to terrain conditions, weather, and physical exertion. Jazmine’s Journey and event organizers are not responsible for any injuries, accidents, or lost items during the hike.
No refunds. All registration fees are considered a donation.

Register today and be part of something impactful! Steele Children’s Research Center is Arizona’s premier pediatric research institution, dedicated to advancing medical discoveries that improve the lives of children. As part of the University of Arizona College of Medicine, the center focuses on groundbreaking research in pediatric cancer, autoimmune diseases, genetics, and more, with the goal of developing better treatments and ultimately finding cures.
Their team of world-class physicians and scientists work tirelessly to transform research into real-life solutions, ensuring that children receive the most innovative and effective care possible. By supporting Steele Children’s Research Center, we are helping to create a future where no child has to face life-threatening illnesses alone.

Learn more about their mission at https://peds.arizona.edu/research
