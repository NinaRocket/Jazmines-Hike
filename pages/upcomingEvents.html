Jazmine’s Journey Annual Blood Drive ❤️🩸

Partnering with the American Red Cross

Location: 1st Southern Baptist Church (Heart of Mesa), 851 N Stapley Dr, Mesa, AZ 85203

Jazmine’s Journey Annual Blood Drive – Save the Date!

We are honored to host our Annual Blood Drive on Saturday, May 9th, 2026 in partnership with the American Red Cross.

During Jazmine’s treatment, she needed countless blood product transfusions. Blood donors helped keep her alive. They gave us more time. More hugs. More memories.

There are so many children, just like Jazmine, who are fighting cancer right now and relying on blood and platelet transfusions to survive. Your donation can truly be the difference between life and death for a child and their family.

Please consider signing up for a time slot today and help us continue Jazmine’s legacy of hope.
