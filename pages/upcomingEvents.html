Jazmine’s Journey Annual Blood Drive ❤️🩸

Partnering with the American Red Cross

Location: 1st Southern Baptist Church (Heart of Mesa), 851 N Stapley Dr, Mesa, AZ 85203

Jazmine’s Journey Annual Blood Drive – Save the Date!

We are honored to host our Annual Blood Drive on Saturday, May 9th, 2026 in partnership with the American Red Cross.

During Jazmine’s treatment, she needed countless blood product transfusions. Blood donors helped keep her alive. They gave us more time. More hugs. More memories.

There are so many children, just like Jazmine, who are fighting cancer right now and relying on blood and platelet transfusions to survive. Your donation can truly be the difference between life and death for a child and their family.

Please consider signing up for a time slot today and help us continue Jazmine’s legacy of hope.

Jazmine’s Journey Hike for Pediatric Cancer Research 🥾💛

Location: North Mountain Visitor Center, 12950 N. 7th Street, Phoenix, AZ 85022

Join us for a meaningful community hike in honor of Jazmine and in support of pediatric cancer research. This special event brings families, friends, and supporters together to raise critical funds and awareness while enjoying the beauty of North Mountain.

This is more than just a hike — this is a hike where we are taking steps for Jazmine that she couldn’t, as she was just learning how to walk when she passed away.

All funds raised through this event will be donated directly to Steele Children’s Research, supporting life-saving pediatric cancer research in Jazmine’s honor.

Whether you hike the entire trail or join us to show support, your participation helps fuel research, bring hope to families, and honor the strength of children fighting cancer.
