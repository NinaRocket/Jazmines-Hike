Pediatric Cancer Families Free Event Attendance Policy 💛

Jazmine’s Journey proudly provides free events and experiences for Arizona pediatric cancer families through the generosity of donors, sponsors, and community supporters. Because space, supplies, and funding are limited, registration spots are reserved specifically for families who plan to attend.

If your family is no longer able to attend an event, we kindly ask that you notify Jazmine’s Journey as soon as possible so the opportunity may be offered to another family.

Please note that repeated no call/no show attendance may result in limited eligibility for future free Jazmine’s Journey events and programs. This policy helps ensure that available resources are used responsibly and that as many pediatric cancer families as possible have the opportunity to participate.
