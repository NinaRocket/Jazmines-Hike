Jazmine’s Journey is donating feeding tube covers and port covers to pediatric cancer patients currently in treatment. The pictures on the Zeffy Form are the ones we currently have on hand. These covers have been purchased from Milton & Maisie on Etsy. You can browse their store at https://www.etsy.com/shop/MiltonandMaisie?ref=shop-header-name&listing_id=714050312&from_page=listing

How to Request a Cover:
1️⃣ Choose ONE cover you would like to receive.
2️⃣ Email documentation proving current treatment to info@jazminesjourney.com.
Accepted documents include:
- A letter from a social worker
- A treatment plan that includes the child’s name
3️⃣ Once verified, you will receive an email confirming approval and an estimated shipping date.

⚠️ Important Information:
Filling out this form does NOT guarantee approval. Please ensure all information provided is correct and up to date. Requests without proper documentation will not be processed.
