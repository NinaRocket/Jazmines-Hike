Taking steps in her memory

Miles for Jazmine

Honor Jazmine’s strength and spirit through a meaningful movement challenge created to raise awareness, support pediatric cancer families, and continue her unseen journey.

Join the Challenge

30 Miles in 30 Days

Our Miles for Jazmine challenge is simple: complete 30 miles in 30 days.

You can walk, run, bike, swim, hike, use adaptive movement, or move in whatever way works best for your body. This challenge is about honoring Jazmine, supporting pediatric cancer families, and taking each step with purpose.

Every mile is a tribute. Every step makes a difference.

A simpler way to participate

Miles for Jazmine was originally created as a web app, but we are moving to a simple downloadable PDF challenge tracker for now.

This helps Jazmine’s Journey keep costs low while still giving families, supporters, and community members a meaningful way to participate from anywhere.

No account needed. No app required. Just download, move, track, and honor Jazmine.

How It Works

The challenge is simple, meaningful, and flexible so you can participate in a way that works for you.

1

Register

Sign up for the Miles for Jazmine Challenge through Zeffy.

2

Track Your Miles

Receive your mileage log sheet and complete 30 miles in 30 days by walking, running, hiking, biking, swimming, or moving your way.

3

Get Recognized

Submit your completed log sheet and be recognized on the Jazmine’s Journey website as a Miles for Jazmine finisher.

Why Miles for Jazmine Matters

Jazmine’s Journey exists to support families impacted by pediatric cancer and to honor Jazmine’s life, strength, and legacy. Miles for Jazmine is one more way we can carry her story forward.

Whether you complete your miles alone, with friends, with your family, at the gym, on a trail, around your neighborhood, or one small step at a time, your participation helps bring awareness to children and families facing the unimaginable.

You are not just logging miles. You are taking steps Jazmine never got the chance to take.

Celebrate Your Finish

When you complete your 30 miles, send us your finished log sheet so we can celebrate your accomplishment. Finishers will be recognized on the Jazmine’s Journey website as part of our Miles for Jazmine community.

Your miles matter. Your effort matters. And we would love to celebrate you.
