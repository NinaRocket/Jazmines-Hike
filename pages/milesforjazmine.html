Miles for Jazmine: Her Unseen Journey to Cure Pediatric Cancer 💛

Join us in honoring Jazmine’s strength and spirit through Miles for Jazmine — a movement-based challenge series created to raise awareness and funds for pediatric cancer. Her journey was one of courage, love, and unseen battles, and now, we walk in her footsteps to continue the fight.
Our first challenge: 30 Miles in 30 Days.

Walk, run, bike, swim — move in whatever way works for you — and complete 30 miles over 30 days to support children and families facing pediatric cancer. Every mile is a tribute. Every step makes a difference.
