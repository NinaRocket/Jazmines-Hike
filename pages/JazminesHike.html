Jazmine's Hike: Taking steps in her memory



Join us for Jazmine's Hike on October 12, 2024! This event is dedicated to supporting Jazmine's Journey in our mission and her unseen journey to cure pediatric cancers.

The check-in will begin at 7 am at North Mountain Visitor Center. We will be using TRAIL 100. Remember to drink plenty of water the day prior and the day of the hike. Bring sunscreen, a hat, and dress accordingly.

Event Details:

  • Date: October 12, 2024
  • Time: Starting at 7:00 AM
  • Location: 12950 N 7th St, Phoenix, AZ 85022 (North Mountain Visitor Center)

For those who can't join us in person, we are also offering a Virtual Hike option. Participate from anywhere and still be part of the fun! We will have a Zoom Link that will be emailed out 48 hours prior to the hike with all of the details.
