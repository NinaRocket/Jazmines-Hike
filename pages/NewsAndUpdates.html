Jazmine’s Parade – A Community’s Love in Her Final Days

In 2020, during Jazmine’s final week at home, our community came together to show their love and support in the most beautiful way—through a parade just for her. Cars lined the streets, filled with friends, family, and strangers who became family, all there to celebrate her strength, her smile, and the love she brought into the world.

Though her time with us was far too short, Jazmine’s impact is everlasting. This moment is a testament to the kindness and compassion that surrounded her, and it’s a reminder of why we continue her journey today through Jazmine’s Journey to support families, spread awareness, and fight for a cure for pediatric cancer.

💛 Forever in our hearts, always in our mission.
