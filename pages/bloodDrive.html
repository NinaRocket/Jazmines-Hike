🩸 Jazmine’s Journey Blood Drive – In Honor of Jazmine 💛

We’re honored to host our 2nd Blood Drive in memory of sweet Jazmine, who passed away from infant leukemia at just 16 months old.

Jazmine needed countless blood transfusions throughout her treatment. Each one gave her more time and comfort—and now, your donation can do the same for another child fighting cancer. I hope you will consider donating in Jazmine's honor and share with your friends and family.

The need for blood is constant and only volunteer donors can fulfill that need for patients in our community. Nationwide, someone needs a unit of blood every 2 to 3 seconds and most of us will need blood in our lifetime.
