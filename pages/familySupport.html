Free Family Photo Sessions – In Partnership with A Full Moon Photography

Jazmine’s Journey is proud to partner with A Full Moon Photography to provide FREE professional family photo sessions for pediatric cancer families currently undergoing treatment.
We know how important it is to capture special moments of joy, love, and togetherness during such a challenging time. Through this partnership, families will have the opportunity to create lasting memories at no cost.

How It Works:

  • Families register through our Zeffy link.
  • After registering, you will automatically receive a thank-you email (please check your spam folder).
  • That email will include a link to contact A Full Moon Photography directly.
  • Once you reach out, A Full Moon Photography will connect with you to schedule your family’s session (date, time, and location).

This process allows both Jazmine’s Journey and A Full Moon Photography to keep track of registrations and ensure every family receives their session.

💛 We are so grateful for this partnership and the opportunity to bring joy and comfort to families when it’s needed most.

We understand that life can be unpredictable, and if you need to cancel, please let us know as soon as possible so we can offer the spot to another parent. Space is limited, so be sure to reserve your spot early!We can’t wait to share this calming experience with you! 🙏💙
