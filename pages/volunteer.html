Join Our New Volunteer Committee

Jazmine’s Journey is excited to announce the launch of our Volunteer Committee! This committee will play a vital role in helping us grow our impact by recruiting volunteers, supporting our events, and building a strong community of advocates for pediatric cancer families. 💛

We are currently seeking passionate and dedicated individuals to lead and serve on this new committee. If you have a heart for service and want to make a difference in the lives of children and families affected by pediatric cancer, we’d love for you to join us.

As part of the Volunteer Committee, you will:

  • Help recruit and organize volunteers for events like our Easter Basket Drive, Polar Express Movie Night, and Pickleball Tournament.
  • Support outreach efforts to raise awareness and engage the community.
  • Shape the future of volunteer involvement with Jazmine’s Journey.

Message amber@jazminesjourney.com to apply!

Together, we can honor Jazmine’s story and bring hope, joy, and strength to families in the fight against pediatric cancer. 💛
