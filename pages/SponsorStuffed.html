About the Program:

🧸 Jazmine’s Journey

Adopt a Stuffed Animal Program

✨ Directions ✨

  1. Pick Out Your Favorite Stuffed Animal
    Choose from the selection of cuddly stuffed animals at our table.
  2. Create a Name
    Think of a special name for your new friend.
  3. Make a Birth Certificate
    Fill out and decorate your stuffed animal’s very own birth certificate to make it official!
  4. Take Home a Gift
    Don’t forget to grab your Jazmine’s Journey Coloring Book & Crayons to continue the fun at home.

✨ Bring Comfort and Joy for Only $15! ✨

We invite you to join Jazmine’s Journey in bringing comfort and smiles to children battling pediatric cancer. For just $15, you can sponsor a stuffed animal package that includes:

  • 🧸 A stuffed animal
  • 🖍️ A coloring book and crayons
  • 🎒 A take-home bag

At our events, children undergoing treatment can stop by our booth to:

  • Choose their very own stuffed animal
  • Create a personalized birth certificate for it
  • Enjoy the extra fun of coloring

💝 Special Option: Sponsor Jazmine’s Piggie

Jazmine’s Piggie was her cherished companion during treatment, providing comfort during difficult procedures and helping her feel safe at night. Sponsoring Jazmine’s Piggie not only comforts a child but also honors Jazmine’s memory.

📝 Recognition for Donors

On the certificate for the sponsored stuffed animal, you may choose to include “Sponsored by (your name)” as a personal touch.

🌟 Why Sponsor?

Stuffed animals offer comfort, companionship, and courage to children during treatment. Your donation makes a difference in their journey, reminding them they’re not alone.
