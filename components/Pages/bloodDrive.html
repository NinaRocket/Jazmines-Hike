Jazmine's Blood Drive

During Jazmine's Cancer treatments, she went through so many blood product transfusions that I can't even keep track of. By donating blood, you will be helping other children just like Jazmine who are in need of countless blood transfusions. You will be saving lives. I hope you will consider donating in Jazmine's honor and share with your friends and family. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out so I can assist in answering them.

The need for blood is constant and only volunteer donors can fulfill that need for patients in our community. Nationwide, someone needs a unit of blood every 2 to 3 seconds and most of us will need blood in our lifetime.
