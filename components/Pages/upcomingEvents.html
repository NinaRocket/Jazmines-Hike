September 21st

CHAMP Walk

Location: Kiwanis Park, North Soccer Field, Tempe, AZ

Walk in Honor of Jazmine

We invite you to join us in honoring the memory of Jazmine by participating in the CHAMP Walk. By walking for Jazmine, you are not only celebrating her brave journey but also showing your support for all CHAMPIONS who have faced pediatric cancer. Your participation will help raise awareness and crucial funds for pediatric cancer research and support services.
Additionally, a portion of the sign-up registrations will be donated to Jazmine's Journey, directly contributing to our mission of curing pediatric cancers and providing essential resources to families in need. Let's come together as a community, walk in unity, and make a meaningful impact in Jazmine's honor.
Be sure to stop by Jazmine’s Journey booth and say hello. Jazmine’s family will be participating in the walk.
  • 6:30AM - 5K Run/Walk Day of Registration
  • 7:00AM - Booth Fair
  • 7:15AM - Opening Ceremony
  • 7:30AM - 5K Start
  • 7:45AM - Breakfast
  • 9:15AM - CHAMPION Walk (On treatment, in remission, and those walking in honor of a child)
  • Awards (immediately following walk)
  • Raffle & Silent Auction
