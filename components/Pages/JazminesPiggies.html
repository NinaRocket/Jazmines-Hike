Jazmine's Journey

Jazmine's Piggie Fund

Jazmine's Journey is raising funds to purchase Taggies Piggies to donate to local children's hospitals. Jazmine is a little girl who was diagnosed at 6 months old with Infant Leukemia. On May 5, 2020, she passed away in her mother's arms in the comfort of her home surrounded by her family.

Jazmine had a stuffed taggie animal piggie. This piggie provided her comfort when she went in for her medical procedures including her chemotherapy treatment. Due to the piggie bringing Jazmine so much joy and comfort, we would like to donate the same piggies to other children battling cancer just like Jazmine. We are reaching out to the community for donations to purchase these piggies. Thanks to the company, Mary Meyer , they are allowing us to purchase them at wholesale cost.
