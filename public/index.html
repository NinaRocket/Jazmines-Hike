Mission Statement

At Jazmine’s Journey, our mission is to honor the life of Jazmine, a young and courageous soul, who touched the lives of many during her battle with infant leukemia. We are passionately dedicated to taking steps towards finding a cure for pediatric cancers by organizing meaningful hiking challenges that serve as powerful fundraising platforms for cutting edge research and family support.

Inspired by Jazmine’s unwavering smile and brave spirit, these challenges turn grief into action and the hope of finding a cure into a reality. We invite everyone from around the world to join these challenges and visit places that she was not able to see, to leave a legacy of hope and healing in Jazmine’s name.
